How to take care of a New Born Baby?

  • Make your baby feel comfortable (i.e.,) the same way he/she felt inside your womb.
  • Feed your baby atleast for every 3 hours
  • Before feeding mother needs to drink 1 glass of water
  • Wipe your baby every day, and baby needs to take her first bath after 48 hours
  • Soak baby clothes in Dettol water for 30 minutes make cloths dry in sunlight then use it for baby
  • Keep your baby’s bed clean, and prefer white color bed sheets so that we can be aware of small insects and mosquitoes.

How should a Mother care for herself post pregnancy?

  • Every mother should drink plenty of fluids, which will help to increase milk segregation
  • Another important thing is every new mothers should take rest as much possible because your body will take few more weeks to adapt.
  • Spend quality time with your partner
  • Eat healthy meal atleast 5 times a day
  • Meeting the constant need for newborn involves time and energy. It often takes you away from other responsibilities in home.
